01 / Web Design

Web Design & Development

I design and build websites that feel modern, premium, structured, and ready to convert. Every layout is created with mobile-first responsiveness, clean hierarchy, strong messaging, and professional presentation in mind.

The goal is not only to make a website look good, but to make the business feel stronger, more established, and easier to trust. That means better section flow, clearer calls to action, stronger service positioning, and cleaner front-end execution.

Ideal for businesses that want a more professional online identity, stronger lead flow, better booking or inquiry structure, and a polished digital experience.

What’s Included

  • Custom responsive website design
  • Premium homepage and service page structure
  • Conversion-focused call-to-action flow
  • SEO-ready HTML structure and clean front-end code
  • Dropdowns, forms, animations, and mobile optimization
  • Fast, modern, professional visual presentation
02 / Storefront Media

Storefront Media & Signage

Your storefront is part of your brand. Before customers even open your website, they may see your sign, your exterior name, your front windows, or your visual presentation from the street. That first impression matters.

I provide design direction for storefront media so the physical presentation of the business feels clean, modern, premium, and aligned with the quality of the website. This helps create one clear identity across digital and real-world customer touchpoints.

This service is especially valuable for barbershops, salons, restaurants, retail brands, clinics, and other storefront businesses that need to look stronger from the outside.

This connects your website, exterior identity, and customer-facing physical presentation into one premium business image.

What’s Included

  • Exterior business name presentation direction
  • Front signage concept guidance
  • Window graphic and storefront media ideas
  • Visual alignment with website branding
  • Walk-in first impression strategy
  • Modern premium storefront positioning
03 / Brand Systems

Brand Presentation Systems

A strong brand is not just a logo. It is how the colors, typography, layout, service naming, messaging tone, website sections, and storefront visuals all work together.

I help shape a presentation system that feels intentional and cohesive. That means making sure the website, business terminology, and physical-facing visuals all support the same image: modern, trustworthy, premium, and established.

This is ideal when a business already has some pieces in place, but the full experience still feels disconnected, inconsistent, or underdeveloped.

The result is a cleaner, stronger, more unified business image that improves perception without feeling overdesigned.

What’s Included

  • Visual direction for digital and physical presence
  • Typography, layout, and hierarchy consistency
  • Premium terminology and service naming alignment
  • Color and presentation guidance
  • Website-to-storefront continuity
  • Professional structure across customer touchpoints
04 / Support

Ongoing Updates & Support

Businesses evolve. Services change, visuals are refined, offers get updated, and customer expectations shift. Ongoing support keeps your website and presentation current instead of letting them become outdated.

I provide continued support for content updates, layout improvements, new sections, visual refinements, presentation upgrades, and growth-focused design adjustments so your business keeps looking strong as it scales.

This is ideal for businesses that want a long-term design partner instead of a one-time build with no follow-through.

Support can include website improvements, visual refinements, business presentation updates, and new design assets as needed.

What’s Included

  • Website edits and new section updates
  • Visual refreshes and layout improvements
  • Service content changes and refinements
  • Ongoing support for growth and polish
  • Continued UX improvements
  • Presentation upgrades as the business evolves
